About ArovelCare: Bridging the Healthcare Gap in Bangladesh

Bangladesh, with its vibrant population of over 170 million, faces significant healthcare challenges. Uneven access, limited infrastructure, a shortage of dedicated health professionals, and fragmented data across public and private sectors create a complex landscape. ArovelCare emerges as the essential centralized digital healthcare system designed to address these issues holistically, transforming challenges into opportunities for a healthier nation.

Our mission is to unite 180 million Bangladeshis under a single, comprehensive healthcare system, each with a unique Health ID. We are committed to ensuring that quality care reaches every citizen, from bustling urban centers to the most remote rural villages. ArovelCare empowers healthcare providers with a unified platform for Electronic Health Records (EHRs), virtual consultations, real-time analytics, and more, streamlining workflows and enhancing patient engagement through advanced encryption and AI-driven insights.

Core Features of Our Centralized Platform

Unified Electronic Health Records (EHR)

Centralize all patient data for seamless, secure access by authorized providers nationwide, reducing duplicate tests and ensuring care continuity.

Integrated Telemedicine Hubs

Connect rural patients with urban specialists via secure video consultations, lowering travel costs and bridging the specialist gap in underserved regions.

Real-Time Disease Surveillance

Monitor outbreaks like dengue or cholera using AI-powered analytics, enabling rapid containment and efficient resource allocation during epidemics.

Digital Immunization Tracking

Track vaccinations via mobile apps with automated SMS reminders for parents, significantly boosting immunization coverage and reducing preventable diseases.

Smart Supply Chain Management

Track medicine and vaccine stocks via IoT sensors to prevent shortages, reduce waste, and ensure timely delivery to even the most remote facilities.

AI-Powered Insights & Recommendations

Leverage intelligent care suggestions based on patient history and patterns, improving diagnoses, predicting outbreaks, and optimizing resource allocation.

Multilingual Patient & Provider Interface

Offer comprehensive support in both Bengali and English, ensuring accessibility and ease of use for all citizens and healthcare professionals.

Fraud Detection & Compliance Tools

Implement robust systems to prevent medical fraud, ensure tax compliance, and provide audit trails, saving public funds and improving trust.

Benefits for Every Stakeholder

ArovelCare is designed to uplift the entire healthcare ecosystem in Bangladesh.

For Patients

Complete Health Record Access

Patients gain secure access to their full medical history, empowering them to participate actively in their care decisions.

Reduced Redundancy & Errors

Eliminates unnecessary repeat tests and procedures, saving time and money, and minimizing risks from fragmented information.

Seamless Continuity of Care

Ensures consistent, high-quality care regardless of location or provider, making transitions smoother and more effective.

For Doctors & Healthcare Providers

Informed Decision-Making

Access to comprehensive patient histories enables more accurate diagnoses and treatment plans, especially for complex cases.

Streamlined Workflows

Reduces paperwork and administrative burdens, allowing doctors to focus more on patient care and less on manual tasks.

Enhanced Collaboration

Facilitates secure and efficient communication with specialists and other providers, leading to better coordinated patient outcomes.

For Pharmacies, Labs & Diagnostic Centers

Direct Digital Prescriptions

Prescriptions are sent directly from doctors, increasing accuracy, reducing errors, and streamlining dispensing processes.

Efficient Inventory & Sales

Integrated tools for inventory management, barcode scanning, and real-time sales tracking optimize operations and reduce waste.

Legal Protection & Compliance

All transactions are legally backed by the system, ensuring accountability and preventing issues like self-medication misuse.

For the Government & Public Health

Real-Time Public Health Monitoring

Enables immediate surveillance of disease outbreaks and provides critical insights for targeted public health interventions.

Improved Resource Allocation

Data-driven insights inform healthcare budgeting and infrastructure planning, ensuring equitable access across the nation.

Enhanced Transparency & Accountability

Full record-keeping of facilities and professionals, coupled with automated processes, reduces bureaucracy and fosters trust.
